##  Project Overview
This repository documents the practical application of the **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) v1.1** to a complex business environment (**Botium Toys**). Moving from a baseline internal audit, I mapped organizational security objectives to the five core NIST functions: **Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.** 

This project demonstrates my ability to translate technical vulnerabilities into a high-level **Risk Management Strategy** that ensures business continuity and regulatory compliance.

##  Framework Execution Summary
*   **IDENTIFY:** Conducted hybrid-asset inventory and quantified risk involving EU-resident PII.
*   **PROTECT:** Engineered a defense-in-depth strategy focusing on **IAM Hardening** and **Data Sovereignty**.
*   **DETECT:** Designed a roadmap for continuous monitoring through **IDS** and **SIEM (ELK Stack)** integration.
*   **RESPOND:** Established incident containment protocols and executive communication escalations.
*   **RECOVER:** Defined resilience requirements for **Disaster Recovery (DRP)** and immutable backup validation.

##  Impact & Business Value
This methodology transforms security from a cost center into a business enabler. By aligning with NIST CSF, I ensured the organization met the rigorous technical requirements for **GDPR** (EU), **PCI DSS** (Global Payments), and **SOC2** (Trust Services).
